How do I change my password or contact email address

You can change your password and email address in the account manager by pressing the account tab above. If you have any trouble don't hesitate to email corporate@acm.uiuc.edu.

How do I download the resume book?

To download the ACM@UIUC resume book, you must first create a download set. This is a filtered set of all the resumes available through our system. To create a download set, click the browse tab above and fill out the form to the filter parameters you wish to view and then click the blue 'create download' button. To create a download of all available resumes, just leave the form blank and click the 'create download' button, and you will get the full, unfiltered set.

How do I get new and updated resumes?

New or updated resumes are immediately available to you through this interface. For download sets that you have already created, you will see an alert that says 10 new or updated. Under this download set you can choose to download all the resumes, including ones that you have previously downloaded, or only the new or updated ones.

When creating a new download set, you will always be given the most up-to-date resumes available for each student.

Other questions

If you have any other questions, please email us at corporate@acm.uiuc.edu.
